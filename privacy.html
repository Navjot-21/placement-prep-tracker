Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection are our top priorities

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, fill out forms, or communicate with us. This includes personal details, academic information, and professional data.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, facilitate placements, communicate important updates, and ensure platform security.

Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Information Sharing

We share your information only with your consent, with recruiters when you apply for positions, and as required by law. We never sell your personal data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also control privacy settings and communication preferences in your account.

Contact for Privacy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@placementhub.com
