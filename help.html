Help & FAQ

Find answers to common questions and get support

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account?

Select your role (Student/TPO/Recruiter) on the login page and enter your details to get started.

For Students

Q: How do I apply for jobs?

Browse available jobs in the Jobs section, review requirements, and click Apply. Make sure your resume is updated first.

For Recruiters

Q: How do I post a job?

Use the Post Job section to create job listings. Fill in all required details and submit for TPO approval.

Technical Support

Q: I'm having login issues

Clear your browser cache, ensure JavaScript is enabled, or contact support if the problem persists.

Account Management

Q: How do I update my profile?

Go to your Profile section and click Edit. Save changes after updating your information.

Contact Support

Need more help?

Reach out to our support team at support@placementhub.com or use the contact form.
